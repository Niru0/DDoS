DDoS Attack

WHAT IS DDoS ATTACK?

DDoS Attack means "Distributed Denial-of-Service (DDoS) Attack"!

HOW IT WORKS?

Thousands of infected devices (botnet) flood a server with traffic, making it crash.

TYPES OF DDoS ATTACK

  • UDP Flood
  • SYN Flood
  • HTTP Flood
