🚨 DDoS Attack

🧠 What is a DDoS Attack?

DDoS stands for "Distributed Denial-of-Service". It's an attack where multiple systems flood a target to crash it.

🔁 How It Works

Thousands of infected devices (a botnet) send massive traffic to a server, overwhelming its resources and causing downtime.

🧨 Types of DDoS Attacks

  • UDP Flood
  • SYN Flood
  • HTTP Flood

💥 Impact

🛑 Website Down
💸 Financial Loss
😡 User Frustration

🛡️ Prevention

  • Use firewalls and rate-limiting
  • Enable anti-DDoS services (e.g., Cloudflare)
  • Continuously monitor network traffic
